January Training Schedule.

January 4-8 – First session of Training for new counselors.This will be at Clark College in the computer science lab, room AA-4102. An additional training day for OR returns and efile certification will be held in Jan at the First Baptist Church in Vancouver, 108 W 27th St.

January 11-12 and Jan 20-21 – Training for returning counselors. This will include Taxwise Lab sessions. Location will be the Clark College computer science lab, room AA-4102 and at the First Baptist Church. Sign up on a first come, first served basis. Additional labs will be held at the church during the 2nd and 3rd weeks in January.

In Vancouver 2 sites will be participating. First Baptist Church and Battle Ground Community Library, 1207 SE 8th, Battle Ground. Contact Ron Sjolander to participate.
