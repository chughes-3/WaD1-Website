The AARP Tax-Aide Organization in Washington

The AARP Tax-Aide program in Washington State involves over 193 sites divided over 22 districts that cover the state. Virtually all sites are focussed on e-filing Tax returns. There are some sites that do paper returns, for those taxpayers who still prefer paper returns

Washington State is a part of the North West Region (Esther Immer, Regional Coordinator, is also based in Washington State) which in turn is one of several regions across the United States
