Serving taxpayers in Clark and Skamania Counties, including the Vancouver Metropolitan area (Vancouver, Camas, Battle Ground, Hazel Dell, and Salmon Creek) and Cowlitz and Wahkiakum Counties, including the Longview/Kelso area

Welcome to the AARP Tax-Aide web site for SW Washington State area. AARP Tax-Aide is the nation’s largest free, volunteer-run tax assistance and preparation service available to low and middle-income taxpayers, with special focus on seniors and those eligible for the Earned Income Credit.

Taxpayer Information

The Locations or Sites pages provide information about where our free tax preparation services are provided, and when these sites are open. Our sites will reopen February 1, 2011 for the 2010 tax year. Taxpayers will find information about our free tax preparation services under the Taxes menu. There are 5 sub-headings in this list. They include help finding a site, current tax issues, and Frequently asked questions, such as “What do I bring?”

Introduction to the Program

The AARP Tax-Aide program for SW Washington covers Clark and Cowlitz counties encompassing the greater Vancouver area, and Skamania and Wahkiakum counties including the Longview,Kelso and Cathlamet areas. Tax preparation sites are in the greater Vancouver area and the Longview/Kelso area. An outline of the history of the program can be found on the About us page. Information about how the process by which we complete and submit our clients' tax returns can be found on the Program Operation page.

Information for Volunteers

Volunteers can find timely information related to the tasks they perform. This includes Training, and Site locations and hours. Special password protected pages contain information such as local organization charts and volunteer position descriptions for Active Volunteers and Leadership personnel.

Persons interested in volunteering can fill out the Online Form. Completing this form will automatically cause your information to be sent to our local area so that you can be contacted.
